About Petal & Stem

At Petal&Stem, we believe flowers are more than just beautiful arrangements—they’re a way to express what words sometimes can’t. Whether it’s love, gratitude, celebration, or comfort, our blooms are thoughtfully designed to help you share life’s most meaningful moments. Founded with a passion for natural beauty and intentional design, Petal&Stem brings together fresh, seasonal flowers sourced from trusted growers. Every bouquet is carefully arranged by hand, blending color, texture, and fragrance into compositions that feel both timeless and personal.

Our Philosophy

We focus on simplicity, elegance, and authenticity. Rather than overcomplicating design, we let each flower speak for itself.

What we offer

From everyday bouquets to custom arrangements for weddings, events, and special occasions, Petal&Stem is here to help you celebrate life in bloom.

Our Promise

Every order is handled with care, creativity, and attention to detail. We don’t just deliver flowers—we deliver emotion, wrapped in petals and stems.
